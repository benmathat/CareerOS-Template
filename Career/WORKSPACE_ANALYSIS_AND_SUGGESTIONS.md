## Executive Summary

This workspace demonstrates **excellent architectural thinking** with clear separation of concerns, thoughtful folder structure, and well-designed templates. The system successfully balances strategic thinking with tactical execution. Below are suggestions to enhance usability, completeness, and long-term effectiveness.

---

## Strengths

1. **Clear separation of concerns** - The distinction between "truth" (work experience) and "presentation" (resumes) is well-articulated
2. **Pipeline thinking** - The folder structure maps naturally to the job search process
3. **Dual control system** - `career-index.md` (strategic) vs `search-status.md` (tactical) is sophisticated
4. **Comprehensive templates** - STAR, SOAR, project templates provide good structure
5. **Archive philosophy** - Nothing deleted, only deactivated preserves history
6. **Well-documented** - Each folder has clear README explaining purpose
