# Self Reflection

This folder contains your personal reflections and observations.

## Purpose

Capture your own insights and learnings:
- Reflections on interviews and applications
- Self-assessments and observations
- Patterns you notice in your behavior or results
- Personal growth and development notes
- Lessons learned and insights

## How to Use

- Create reflection documents regularly
- Document what you're learning about yourself
- Note patterns in your experiences
- Track personal growth and development
- Use to inform future decisions and approaches

This folder helps you develop self-awareness and learn from your experiences.
