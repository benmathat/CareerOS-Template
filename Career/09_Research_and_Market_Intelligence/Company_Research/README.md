# Company Research

This folder contains deep dives on specific companies.

## Purpose

Research companies you're interested in or applying to:
- Company history and background
- Business model and revenue streams
- Products, services, and market position
- Recent news, funding, or changes
- Culture, values, and employee experience
- Leadership and organizational structure

## How to Use

- Create one file per company you're researching
- Gather information from multiple sources
- Note what makes the company interesting or concerning
- Reference when preparing for interviews
- Update as you learn more through the process

This folder helps you ask informed questions and demonstrate genuine interest.
