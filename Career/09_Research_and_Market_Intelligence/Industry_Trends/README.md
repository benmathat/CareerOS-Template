# Industry Trends

This folder contains research on industry trends and developments.

## Purpose

Stay informed about your target industries:
- Industry trends and developments
- Market shifts and disruptions
- Emerging technologies or practices
- Industry challenges and opportunities
- Future outlook and predictions

## How to Use

- Save articles, reports, and analysis
- Note key trends and their implications
- Track how trends affect your target roles
- Reference when preparing for interviews
- Update regularly to stay current

This folder helps you speak knowledgeably about industry context during interviews.
