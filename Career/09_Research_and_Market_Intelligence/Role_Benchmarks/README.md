# Role Benchmarks

This folder contains research on what similar roles require and involve.

## Purpose

Understand what your target roles typically require:
- Common qualifications and requirements
- Typical responsibilities and expectations
- Required vs. preferred skills
- Career progression paths
- How roles vary by company or industry

## How to Use

- Research multiple job postings for similar roles
- Identify patterns in requirements and expectations
- Compare against your own experience and skills
- Identify skill gaps to address
- Reference when tailoring applications and resumes

This folder helps you understand how to position yourself for target roles.
