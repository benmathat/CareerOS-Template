# Master Resume – Source of Truth

## Purpose
This folder contains the **canonical, complete record** of my professional experience.
Nothing is deleted for being “too long” or “off-brand.”

This is the **input** for:
- Tailored resumes
- Cover letters
- LinkedIn optimization
- Interview preparation

---

## What Belongs Here
- Full work history
- All roles and titles
- Expanded bullet points
- Metrics and outcomes
- Projects that didn’t make the final resume
- Experiments, failures, and recoveries

## What Does NOT Belong Here
- Job-specific tailoring
- Keyword stuffing
- Design/layout experimentation

---

## Recommended Structure

```text
Resume_Master/
├── resume-master.md
├── role-bullets-expanded.md
├── metrics-and-impact.md
└── career-timeline.md
```

## Editing Rules
- Optimize for accuracy and completeness
- Use clear, factual language
- Prefer numbers and outcomes over responsibilities
- No concern for length
