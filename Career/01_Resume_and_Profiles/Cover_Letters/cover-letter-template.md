# Cover Letter: <Company> – <Role>

## Purpose

This template provides a structured format for writing cover letters that demonstrate alignment between your experience and the role's requirements. Use it to create compelling, tailored cover letters that complement your resume.

## When to Use

- When applying to roles that require or accept cover letters
- For applications where a cover letter can differentiate you
- When you have a referral or specific connection to highlight
- For roles where written communication is important
- When you want to explain career transitions or gaps
