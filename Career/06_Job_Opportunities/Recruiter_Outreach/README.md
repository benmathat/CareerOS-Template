# Recruiter Outreach

This folder tracks inbound recruiter messages and outreach.

## Purpose

Capture and evaluate recruiter contacts:
- Inbound recruiter messages
- LinkedIn messages and connection requests
- Email outreach from recruiters
- Initial conversations and screening calls

## How to Use

- Log each recruiter contact with date and source
- Note the role, company, and initial impression
- Track follow-up conversations
- Decide whether to pursue or archive
- Move promising opportunities to `07_Applications_and_Interviews/` when appropriate

This folder helps you manage recruiter relationships and identify quality opportunities.
