# Recruiter Outreach Log

## Purpose

This template helps you track inbound recruiter messages and conversations. Use it to evaluate opportunities, maintain recruiter relationships, and decide which opportunities to pursue.

## When to Use

- When receiving recruiter outreach (LinkedIn, email, etc.)
- After initial conversations with recruiters
- When evaluating recruiter-presented opportunities
- To track recruiter relationships over time
- Before deciding whether to apply to recruiter-presented roles
