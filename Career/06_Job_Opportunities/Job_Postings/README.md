# Job Postings

This folder contains saved job postings before you decide to apply.

## Purpose

Store interesting job postings for evaluation:
- Job descriptions you're considering
- Postings that need more research
- Opportunities to compare against your criteria
- Roles you're not ready to apply to yet

## How to Use

- Save postings here before committing to apply
- Include the posting URL and date saved
- Add notes about why it's interesting
- Review periodically to decide: apply, research more, or archive
- Move to `07_Applications_and_Interviews/` when you decide to apply

This folder prevents premature commitment and allows thoughtful evaluation.
