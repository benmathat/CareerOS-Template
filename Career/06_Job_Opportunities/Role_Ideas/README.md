# Role Ideas

This folder contains exploratory role concepts and ideas.

## Purpose

Capture role ideas and concepts you're exploring:
- Role types you're considering
- Career directions you're exploring
- Hybrid or non-traditional roles
- Roles that might not exist yet but should

## How to Use

- Document role ideas as they occur to you
- Research what these roles typically involve
- Identify companies that might have similar roles
- Explore whether these roles align with your goals
- Develop into concrete targets or archive if not viable

This folder supports exploration and helps you think creatively about your career options.
