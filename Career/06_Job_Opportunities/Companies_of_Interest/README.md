# Companies of Interest

This folder tracks companies you're interested in, regardless of current openings.

## Purpose

Maintain a watchlist of companies:
- Companies you admire or want to work for
- Organizations aligned with your values
- Companies in your target industry or market
- Employers with strong culture or reputation

## How to Use

- Create one file per company of interest
- Note why the company is interesting
- Track any openings or opportunities
- Research company culture, values, and recent news
- Move to `07_Applications_and_Interviews/` when an opportunity arises

This folder helps you stay aware of opportunities at companies you care about.
