**Status Transition Reminders:**

- **After Recruiter Screen:** Prepare for Hiring Manager interview (research role, prepare stories)
- **After Hiring Manager:** Prepare for Panel/Onsite (deep company research, technical prep)
- **After Panel/Onsite:** Send thank-you notes within 24 hours, follow up in 1 week
- **After Final:** Prepare for offer negotiation (research compensation, identify priorities)
- **If On Hold > 2 weeks:** Follow up or consider withdrawing
- **If Rejected:** Capture feedback, update story inventory if needed, archive after reflection
