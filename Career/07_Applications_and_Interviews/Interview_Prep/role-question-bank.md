# Role-Based Question Bank

## Leadership
- Tell me about a time you led through ambiguity.
- Describe a difficult decision you had to make with incomplete data.
- How do you handle underperformance?

## Product / Strategy
- How do you decide what not to build?
- Describe a time you changed strategy mid-flight.
- How do you measure success?

## Technical (if applicable)
- Tell me about a system you scaled.
- A technical decision you regret.
- Tradeoffs you considered.

## Conflict & Communication
- A time you disagreed with leadership.
- A difficult stakeholder relationship.
- How you handle pushback.

## Failure & Learning
- A failure you own.
- What you learned.
- What you’d do differently.
