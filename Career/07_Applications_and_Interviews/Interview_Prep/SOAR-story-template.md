# SOAR Story – <Short Title>

## Purpose

This template helps you structure leadership and strategic stories using the SOAR format (Situation, Objective, Action, Result). Use it for senior roles, leadership interviews, and situations requiring strategic thinking and ownership.

## When to Use

- When preparing for leadership or executive interviews
- For senior/strategic role interviews
- When discussing ambiguous problems or strategic decisions
- After leading a significant initiative or project
- When demonstrating ownership and accountability
