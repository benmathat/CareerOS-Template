# Interview Day Checklist

## Before
- ☐ Review job description
- ☐ Review company research
- ☐ Select 5–7 core stories
- ☐ Prepare role-specific questions
- ☐ Confirm interviewer names & roles
- ☐ Test tech / environment

## During
- ☐ Listen for the real question
- ☐ Clarify before answering if needed
- ☐ Lead with outcome when senior
- ☐ Watch time — don’t over-answer
- ☐ Ask thoughtful questions

## After
- ☐ Write interview notes immediately
- ☐ Send thank-you email
- ☐ Log feedback signals
- ☐ Identify improvement areas
