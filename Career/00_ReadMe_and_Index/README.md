# Orientation & Control

> **For an overview of the entire Career workspace, see [`../README.md`](../README.md).**  
> This file focuses specifically on the `00_ReadMe_and_Index/` folder.

This folder is the **control plane** for the entire Career workspace.

Its purpose is to:
- Establish current focus and priorities
- Provide navigation into active materials
- Prevent loss of context over time

This is where you orient yourself when:
- Restarting a search
- Feeling scattered
- Reviewing progress
- Pausing or resuming activity

## What Belongs Here

- Current search status
- Indexes and summaries
- The rolling activity log

If this folder is neglected, the rest of the system will drift.

This folder should be reviewed regularly.

## How `career-index.md` and `search-status.md` Work Together

These two files form the **orientation and control system** for the Career workspace.  
They serve different time horizons and should not be merged or treated as redundant.

---

### `career-index.md` — Strategic Anchor

**Purpose:**  
`career-index.md` provides a **stable, high-level view** of your career search.

It answers questions like:
- What roles am I targeting?
- What searches are active?
- Where are the key materials?
- Who are the important people involved?

**How it is used:**
- As the starting point when opening the Career folder
- To re-orient after a break
- To explain your search to a coach, recruiter, or advisor
- To maintain continuity over weeks or months

**Change frequency:** Low  
This file should evolve slowly and remain valid over time.

---

### `search-status.md` — Tactical Control Panel

**Purpose:**  
`search-status.md` captures your **current operating reality**.

It answers questions like:
- What phase am I in right now?
- What am I focused on this week?
- Where is momentum or friction?
- What should I do next?

**How it is used:**
- Weekly planning and check-ins
- Regaining focus when overwhelmed
- Tracking short-term priorities and blockers
- Honest self-assessment during execution

**Change frequency:** High  
This file is expected to change frequently and reflect reality, not aspiration.

---

### The Key Distinction

> **If the information would still be true in a month, it belongs in `career-index.md`.**  
> **If it might be false next week, it belongs in `search-status.md`.**

This rule prevents duplication and keeps each file effective.

---

### Why Both Are Necessary

Together, these files:
- Prevent reactive or chaotic job searching
- Separate long-term direction from short-term action
- Make it easy to pause and restart without losing context
- Improve the quality of decisions and follow-through

Use `career-index.md` to stay oriented.  
Use `search-status.md` to stay honest and focused.

Both are required for the system to work.
