# Mentors and Advisors

This folder tracks your mentors, advisors, and career guides.

## Purpose

Maintain relationships with mentors and advisors:
- List of mentors and advisors
- Relationship context and history
- Areas where they provide guidance
- Meeting notes and advice received
- How to engage with each person

## How to Use

- Create files for each mentor or advisor
- Document how you met and the relationship
- Track meetings, advice, and key insights
- Note their expertise and how they help you
- Maintain regular contact and show appreciation

This folder helps you leverage mentorship relationships for career growth.
