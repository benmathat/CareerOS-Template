# Contacts

This folder contains your general professional network contacts.

## Purpose

Maintain your professional network:
- Professional contacts and connections
- People you've met at events or through work
- Industry contacts and peers
- Contacts by industry, company, or expertise area

## How to Use

- Create files or notes for key contacts
- Track how you met and relationship context
- Note their role, company, and expertise
- Record last contact date and follow-up needs
- Maintain relationships over time

This folder helps you maintain and leverage your professional network.
