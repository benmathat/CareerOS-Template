# Career Workspace Review Guide

This guide helps you systematically review all important documents in the Career workspace before finalizing the structure. Follow this checklist to ensure everything is complete and correct.

## How to Use This Guide

1. **Work through sections in order** - They're organized from foundational to specific
2. **Check off items** as you review them
3. **Note any issues** in the "Notes" section for each document
4. **Fix issues immediately** or document them for later
5. **Mark sections complete** when finished

**Estimated time:** 2-3 hours for thorough review
