# Values Assessment

## Purpose

This template helps you identify and document your core values and principles. Use it to ensure career decisions align with what matters most to you, evaluate company culture fit, and maintain authenticity in your career.

## When to Use

- When clarifying what matters most in your career
- Before evaluating opportunities (check values alignment)
- After experiencing misalignment (learn what to avoid)
- During company research (assess culture fit)
- When making difficult career decisions
