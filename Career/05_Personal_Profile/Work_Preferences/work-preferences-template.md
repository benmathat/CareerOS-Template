# Work Preferences

## Purpose

This template helps you document your work style preferences and requirements. Use it to clarify what you need to be successful, evaluate opportunities for fit, and communicate preferences during interviews and negotiations.

## When to Use

- When starting a job search (clarify your requirements)
- Before evaluating opportunities (check against preferences)
- After learning from past roles (update based on experience)
- During offer evaluation (ensure alignment)
- When negotiating (know your must-haves)
