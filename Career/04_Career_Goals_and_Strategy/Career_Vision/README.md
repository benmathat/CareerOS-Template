# Career Vision

This folder contains your long-term career vision and direction.

## Purpose

Define where you want your career to go over the long term:
- Career aspirations and goals
- Desired trajectory and milestones
- Vision for impact and contribution
- Long-term professional identity

## How to Use

- Create a vision document that describes your ideal career state
- Review and update periodically (quarterly or annually)
- Reference when evaluating opportunities or making career decisions
- Use to maintain focus during job searches

This vision guides strategic decisions and prevents reactive career moves.
