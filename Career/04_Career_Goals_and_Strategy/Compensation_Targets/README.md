# Compensation Targets

This folder contains your compensation research and targets.

## Purpose

Define and track compensation expectations:
- Target salary ranges by role and location
- Total compensation considerations (equity, benefits, etc.)
- Market research and benchmarks
- Negotiation targets and walk-away points

## How to Use

- Research market rates for your target roles
- Set realistic but ambitious targets
- Document your reasoning and data sources
- Update based on market conditions and experience level
- Reference during offer evaluation and negotiation

This folder supports confident negotiation and prevents accepting undervalued offers.
